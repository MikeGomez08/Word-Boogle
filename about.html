Meet the Developers

1. The objective of the game is to collect words to earn points, with longer words granting higher scores.

2. To begin, select the desired difficulty level from three options: easy, medium, or hard.

3. Each difficulty level has its own time limit, with shorter limits applied to more challenging levels.

4. Use the mouse to click or select neighboring letters on the grid and create words. Words can be formed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Words must be a minimum of three letters long.

5. After the time is up, the program stops the game and displays the list of words found by the player. The program checks the validity of each word against a pre-loaded dictionary. Only valid words are counted.

6. After completing a round, players can choose to start a new game with a different random grid of letters or exit the game.
